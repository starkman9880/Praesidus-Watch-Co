The A-2 Origin: our first step into the 1950s
 
Originally inspired by the A-17 Spec that replaced the WW2 A-11 Spec in 1948 - welcome to the atomic age.
Originally designed in collaboration with two Korean War USAF veterans, the A-2 Origin is our new take on the now iconic A-17 Spec watch.
 
Our USAF veterans were originally the ones that asked us to drop the original 24H inner track that made the A-17 look as they found from experience that it only hampered readability.
 
We've kept close to the original hands - most notably on the arrow second hand which we thinned down for a more slick & everyday feel to the watch. You'll be getting maximal precision from the A-2's hands thanks to its Swiss Made Ronda 763 Quartz movement. 
Get yours now
For the A-2 Origin, we've also decided to refresh the look by using some Black Leather or Nylon NATO Straps.These give off a cleaner more modern field watch aesthetic that promotes better wearability on your daily grind. 
 
For the stainless steel case, we've gone with a detailed combo of circular brushed lugs and sandblasted bezel to reduce the watch's overall shine.
Check it out in detail
Interestingly, the A-2 Origin will be probably be our last collection featuring the Swiss Ronda 763 Quartz as we look to transition towards the Ameriquartz 6130 (more info coming soon 😉). In any case, the Ronda 763 has allowed us to provide a rugged, reliable & affordable field watch that can go toe to toe with most other field watches.
